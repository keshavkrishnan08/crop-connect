Problem

Problem

Independent restaurants run on razor-thin margins, and the easy costs are already cut.

  • Full-service restaurants net just 3 to 5%. Almost nothing is left to cut.
  • The one real lever is to charge more for what is on the plate.
  • Local food earns a premium, but sourcing it is a second full-time job.
  • So most kitchens skip it and leave the margin behind.

Solution

Solution

An AI agent runs local sourcing end to end. The restaurant only says what it wants and puts it on the menu.

  • Tell us one ingredient. The agent runs the rest.
  • It finds and vets farms, signs the agreement, delivers weekly, and hands over the provenance.
  • Set up in about 10 minutes. First item is free.
  • The kitchen does two things: ask, and serve.

Business model

Business model

Food passes through at cost. We earn a recurring monthly service fee that scales with usage and rewards longer terms.

  • No markup on food. No cut of sales.
  • Fee = base + per farm + per item. Recurring and expands as a kitchen adds dishes.
  • Term discounts: 10% at 6 months, 20% at 12.
