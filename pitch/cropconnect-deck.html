CropConnect07

Moat

It gets harder to beat the more it runs. The advantage compounds inside each metro.

  • Two-sided density. Vet and contract a city's farms once, and a rival has to rebuild the whole local supply base to compete. More restaurants pull more capacity; more farms mean better fill.
  • Proprietary data. Every contract, delivery, and price trains the agent on local supply, reliability, and demand, so our matches and forecasts beat any generic tool.
  • Switching costs. Once we run a kitchen's sourcing, contracts, deliveries, and margins, replacing us means rebuilding their supply chain by hand.
  • We own the supply side. The farm relationships and the allocation layer make us the demand aggregator the farming platform is built on.
